Beyond Travel | Website Plan

Site Name

Beyond TravelThis name reflects personalized travel planning services that go above and beyond standard options. It's unique, brandable, and aligns with the mission of helping travelors explore more.

Site Purpose

The purpose of Beyond Travel is to promote travel packages, destinations, and concierge booking services for independent travelers, families, and groups. The site will include featured destinations, booking inquiry forms, and client testimonials.

Scenarios

  • What types of travel packages do you offer (cruises, guided tours, family-friendly trips)?
  • How can I get in touch with a travel consultant to start planning my vacation?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #F0F3f5 (light grey) - used for backgrounds and highlights
  • Accent Color: ##0B7A75 (Brighter Green) - used for buttons and backgrounds
  • Accent Color2: #7B2D26 (Rusty Brown) - used for backgrounds, and highlights
  • Accent Color3: #D7C9AA (Muted Yellow) - used for highlights and backgrounds
  • Text Dark: #19535F (Dark Green) - used for texting and border contrasts

Typography

  • Heading Font: Roboto - Chosen for all headings
  • Body Font: sans-serif fallback - for clear paragraph content
