About Utah

Utah

Utah is known for its natural diversity and is home to features ranging from arid deserts with dunes to thriving pine forests in mountain valleys. It is a rugged and geographically diverse state at the convergence of three distinct geological regions: the Rocky Mountains, the Great Basin, and the Colorado Plateau. This image is of my family exploring the slot canyons in Southern Utah. The mountains are covered with light fluffy powder during the winter months due to the dry climent. Utah is known for great skiing because of the snow. The famous Great Salt Lake allows you to float due to the salt content, much like the Dead Sea. In addition, Utah has five national parks: Arches, Bryce Canyon, Canyonlands, Capital Reef, and Zion.
